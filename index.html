Frequently Asked Questions

What we offer?

We value our customers and as such, we provide the best services and facilities to make bookings according to your time schedule. Our services are available at your convenience.

How it works?

You can book for the classes according to your preference in the Join class section. It can be either a day, a week, a month or an year. You can also make a request for special training from our expert trainers.

What if I am not satisfied?

You can book only for a day, or a week as a trial classes to know about the BodyFit Nepal. Also, please write a feedback message to us and we will reach back to you asap.

What are our payment options?

Our payment options include paying at BodyFit Nepal itself when you reach, using either cash or esewa.

How to cancel bookings?

You can cancel by mailing on our address info@bodyfitnepal.com by your registered email address.
